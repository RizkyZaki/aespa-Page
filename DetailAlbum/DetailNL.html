ABOUT

“Next Level” is the second single released by the South Korean girl group, aespa. The single continues the group’s journey in their fight against the fictional villain dubbed as ‘Black Mamba.’ The storyline of the lyrics was contextualized via SM Culture Universe episode 1 which found aespa losing connections with their aes. In “Next Level” the girls travel to ‘KWANGYA’ or the ‘wilderness’ to stop Black Mamba by diving into the next level. The track is a hip-hop-influenced song that interpolates elements of the soundtrack to the 2019, Fast & Furious Presents: Hobbs & Shaw
